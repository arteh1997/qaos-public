# Feature Summary & Talking Points
## Restaurant Inventory Management System

Use these descriptions when presenting features to the client.

---

## Core Features

### 1. Multi-Store Management
**What it does:** Manage inventory across unlimited restaurant locations from a single dashboard.

**Talking Point:**
> "Whether you have 2 stores or 20, everything is managed from one place. Each location has its own inventory levels, but shares the same product catalog for consistency."

**Key Benefits:**
- Centralized oversight of all locations
- Consistent product naming and units across stores
- Compare performance between locations

---

### 2. Daily Stock Counts
**What it does:** Staff record current inventory quantities daily, with automatic variance tracking.

**Talking Point:**
> "Daily counts take minutes, not hours. Staff simply enter what's on the shelf, and the system handles the rest—tracking changes, flagging discrepancies, and updating reports."

**Key Benefits:**
- Draft auto-save prevents lost work
- Search and filter for fast entry
- Low stock items highlighted automatically
- Complete audit trail of who counted what and when

---

### 3. Stock Reception (Delivery Logging)
**What it does:** Track incoming inventory from deliveries with full accountability.

**Talking Point:**
> "When a delivery arrives, quantities are logged immediately. You know exactly what came in, when, and who received it. No more guessing what was delivered last Tuesday."

**Key Benefits:**
- Real-time inventory updates
- Low stock items prioritized in the list
- Notes field for delivery issues
- Driver accountability

---

### 4. PAR Level Alerts
**What it does:** Automatic alerts when inventory drops below minimum threshold.

**Talking Point:**
> "Set your minimum levels once, and the system watches them for you. When stock drops below PAR, it appears on the dashboard immediately—no manual monitoring needed."

**Key Benefits:**
- Proactive rather than reactive
- Prevents stockouts
- Customizable per item, per store
- Export low stock reports for ordering

---

### 5. Role-Based Access Control
**What it does:** Three user roles with specific permissions tailored to job responsibilities.

**Talking Point:**
> "Everyone sees exactly what they need—nothing more, nothing less. Staff focus on counting, Drivers on deliveries, Admins on management. No confusion, no unauthorized access."

**Role Breakdown:**

| Role | Can Do | Cannot Do |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Admin** | Everything | - |
| **Driver** | View all stores, record deliveries, view reports | Manage users, edit inventory catalog, do stock counts |
| **Staff** | View assigned store, do stock counts, view shifts | See other stores, record deliveries, access reports |

---

### 6. Real-Time Dashboard
**What it does:** At-a-glance overview of system health and required actions.

**Talking Point:**
> "The moment you log in, you see what needs attention. Missing counts, low stock, recent activity—everything important is right there on the dashboard."

**Dashboard Elements:**
- Active stores count
- Active users count
- Missing counts (with warning indicator)
- Low stock alerts (with danger indicator)
- Today's activity feed
- Quick action buttons

---

### 7. Comprehensive Reporting
**What it does:** Data exports and summary reports for decision-making.

**Talking Point:**
> "Data drives better decisions. See which stores consistently miss counts, which items run low most often, and export everything to CSV for your own analysis."

**Available Reports:**
- Daily Summary (count completion by store)
- Low Stock Report (items below PAR)
- Stock History (all movements over time)
- CSV exports for external analysis

---

### 8. Mobile-First Design
**What it does:** Full functionality on phones and tablets with touch-optimized interface.

**Talking Point:**
> "Your team doesn't need to be at a computer. Stock counts, deliveries, even management—everything works perfectly on a phone."

**Key Benefits:**
- Responsive design adapts to any screen
- Touch-optimized inputs
- Works in portrait and landscape
- No app download required (but can be installed)

---

### 9. Offline Capability (PWA)
**What it does:** Continue working without internet, sync when connection returns.

**Talking Point:**
> "Walk-in coolers, basements, weak signal areas—no problem. The app works offline and syncs automatically when you're back online."

**Key Benefits:**
- Install to home screen like a native app
- Works without internet connection
- Automatic background sync
- Push notifications (if enabled)

---

### 10. Audit Trail
**What it does:** Complete history of all changes with user attribution.

**Talking Point:**
> "Every change is logged. You can see exactly who did what and when. Complete accountability for inventory movements."

**Tracked Information:**
- User who made the change
- Timestamp of the action
- What changed (quantity, PAR level, etc.)
- Previous and new values

## Objection Handling

**"We already use spreadsheets"**
> "Spreadsheets work, but they don't scale. This system gives you real-time visibility across all locations, automatic alerts, and mobile access—things spreadsheets can't do."

**"Our staff aren't tech-savvy"**
> "The interface is designed for simplicity. Staff see only what they need, with clear labels and minimal steps. Most people are comfortable within minutes."

**"What if the system goes down?"**
> "The system is cloud-hosted with 99.9% uptime. Plus, the offline capability means work continues even if there's a connection issue."

**"We have unique requirements"**
> "The system is configurable—custom categories, store-specific PAR levels, role assignments. If you need something beyond the standard features, we can discuss customization."

**"How long to implement?"**
> "You can be operational within days. Set up your stores, import your inventory, invite your team, and you're ready."
