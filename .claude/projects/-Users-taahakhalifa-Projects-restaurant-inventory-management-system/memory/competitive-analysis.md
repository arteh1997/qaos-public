**What they do:** Restaurant invoice processing and cost management with human-reviewed accuracy.

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Monthly | Annual (10% off) |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| MarginEdge | $330/location | ~$297/location |
| MarginEdge + Freepour | $480/location | ~$432/location |

**Key Features:**
- Invoice processing via photos, email, uploads, scanning, or EDI
- **Hybrid AI + human review** — restaurant-experienced reviewers verify every line item (24-48hr turnaround)
- Item-based product mapping (not vendor-name-based) — switching vendors doesn't require remapping
- Price history tracking and fluctuation alerts
- **Bill Pay included free** — ACH, virtual credit card, physical checks, AutoPay
- 55,000+ vendor payment network
- Vendor statement reconciliation
- Mobile inventory tracking
- Auto-updating count sheets from processed invoices
- Recipe costing with AI-assisted entry, allergen tracking
- Menu engineering (Stars/Puzzles/Plow Horses/Dogs quadrant model)
- Daily controllable P&L statements
- Budget tracking
- Freepour SmartScale add-on for bar inventory (wireless Bluetooth scales, 35,000+ pre-loaded barcodes, milliliter-accurate)
- Commissary support
- Digital recipe display on tablets

**POS Integrations (60+):** Square, Toast, Clover, Lightspeed, Aloha, Revel, Micros, TouchBistro, SpotOn, GoTab, Shopify, and 50+ more.

**Accounting (12+):** QuickBooks Online, QuickBooks Desktop, Sage Intacct, Xero, Compeat, NetSuite, R365, Microsoft Dynamics GP, and more.

**Capterra: 4.6/5** — Ease of Use 4.5, Customer Service 4.5, Features 4.5, Value 4.5

**G2 Support Rating: 8.5/10** (vs xtraCHEF's 3.1/10)

**Common Complaints:**
- $330/month is expensive for single-location restaurants
- Recipe entry is time-consuming and manual
- Onboarding can be lengthy
- UI feels dated in places
- Mobile app needs improvement
- Manual inventory entry is tedious
- Pulling raw data for ad hoc analysis is difficult
- Occasional system lag during peak times

**What makes them different:** The human-reviewed invoice processing is their core differentiator. Pure OCR typically has ~90% character accuracy — on a 50-line invoice, that's 5+ errors cascading through your data. MarginEdge's human review catches what OCR misses. The trade-off is cost ($330/mo) and speed (24-48hrs vs instant).

**Relevance to us:** They're primarily an invoice/AP tool, not a full inventory system. No scheduling, no payroll, no forecasting, no supplier portal. But their invoice processing + bill pay + 60 POS integrations are features we can't match. If we ever add invoice scanning, the human-review approach is the quality standard to beat.

MarketMan: $500 setup. MarginEdge: $250. Orca: $399-599. R365: $500-1,200. We charge $0 upfront and have no lock-in.

Zettle, SumUp, Epos Now, Tevalis — these are the POS systems UK restaurants actually use. US competitors don't integrate with them.

Shift assigned/updated/cancelled, payslip available, PO updates, delivery received, payment receipts, etc. — all with user-configurable preferences. Most competitors have basic or no notifications.

MarginEdge includes free vendor bill pay (ACH, cheques, virtual credit cards). We don't handle any supplier payment flows.

### 7. No market presence
Every competitor has thousands of customers, G2/Capterra reviews, case studies, and social proof. We have zero. This is the single biggest barrier to conversion at any price.

### 8. No tip management
7shifts has deep tip pooling with POS sync. We have no tip management features.

### 9. No hiring / onboarding tools
Homebase has AI job posts, applicant tracking, background checks, handbook builder. We have basic user invites.

### 10. No HACCP / food safety compliance
Apicbase and Modeeri have dedicated food safety modules. We have nothing for HACCP, temperature logging, or compliance.
